===============================
Survey Question Save as Company
===============================
* Save user's answer as its partner company - This will link the user answer partner as 
  the parent company's child
* Optionally save contacts to partner's company. 
* Answer options:
    - Company Name
    - Company Street
    - Company Zip
    - Company City
    - Company Website
